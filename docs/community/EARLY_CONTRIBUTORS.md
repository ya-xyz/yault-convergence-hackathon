# Early Contributors Program

> Version: 0.1 | Date: 2026-03-08 | Status: Working Draft

---

## Purpose

Yault is at an early stage. Before formal funding or a full-time team, the project needs a core group of committed contributors who believe in the vision and are willing to help shape the protocol from the ground up. This document outlines how we plan to recruit, organize, and recognize early participants.

---

## Contributor Roles

### 1. Core Developer

- **Focus**: Smart contract development, frontend/backend engineering, infrastructure
- **Expected Commitment**: Part-time or flexible; project-based contributions welcome
- **Skills**: Solidity, TypeScript, React, Node.js, or related Web3 stack

### 2. Security Reviewer

- **Focus**: Code review, vulnerability research, audit coordination
- **Expected Commitment**: Periodic review sprints aligned with release milestones
- **Skills**: Smart contract security, formal verification, or penetration testing

### 3. Community Organizer

- **Focus**: Social media, documentation, translations, user support
- **Expected Commitment**: Ongoing, part-time
- **Skills**: Technical writing, community management, multilingual communication

### 4. Strategy & Research

- **Focus**: Yield strategy design, tokenomics, regulatory landscape research
- **Expected Commitment**: Advisory or project-based
- **Skills**: DeFi protocol analysis, financial modeling, compliance knowledge

## Recognition & Incentives

We are transparent about current constraints: there is no funding or token allocation at this stage. What we can offer:

- **On-chain attribution**: Contributor addresses will be recorded in protocol governance history
- **Founding contributor status**: Early participants will be recognized as founding members when the project reaches formal launch
- **Future participation rights**: Founding contributors will have priority access to governance roles and contributor incentive programs when they are established
- **Reputation**: Meaningful open-source contributions to a production DeFi protocol

---

## Expectations

- All contributions are voluntary at this stage
- Contributors retain full ownership of their time and can step away at any point
- We value quality over quantity — one thoughtful review is worth more than ten surface-level PRs
- All participants are expected to follow a standard code of conduct (to be formalized)
